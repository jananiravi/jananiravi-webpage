Bio

I am a computational biologist (Dept. of Pathobiology and Diagnostic Investigation, Michigan State University) working on host-pathogen biology, protein sequence-structure-function relationships, and drug repurposing (@cpathogeno). I am interested in quantitative biology, like to code & develop methods, and enjoy being in a collaborative environment addressing interesting biological questions.

I also like teaching statistical data analysis and visualization, and R/Python programming. I recently founded the programming chapter, R-Ladies East Lansing (year in review), and co-host our meetups & workshops! Check out our ß-webpage here.

I love to read most non-fiction genres, sing classical carnatic music & dabble in keyboard, guitar!
